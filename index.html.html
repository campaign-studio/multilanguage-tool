How to use in Braze

        

          
1
Open Braze and create a new email template or campaign.

          
2
In the editor, choose the Drag & Drop editor.

          
3
Drag an HTML block into the email body.

          
4
Paste the tagged HTML (copied on the left) into the block.

          
5
In the left menu, select "Languages" and set up multilanguage with the CSV you'll download below.
